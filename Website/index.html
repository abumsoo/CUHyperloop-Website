What is Hyperloop

Hyperloop is a future high speed transportation concept first proposed in a 2013 white paper by Elon Musk. Capable of transporting cargo and passengers at over 1200 kph, maturation of the technology could revolutionize the transportation and travel industry.
SpaceX hosts an internaltional competition anually where teams compete against each other to design, manufacture, and test their own Hyperloop Pod designs at their facitility in Hawthorn, CA.

About

CU Hyperloop is a team based out of the University of Colorado Boulder made up of approximately 17 undergraduate engineering students. Along with the help of campus mentors, we're competing in the SpaceX Hyperloop Pod Competition.
Our participation in the 2018 competition has officially ended, but we will still be building flight-like prototypes of critical subsystems in order to prepare for the 2019 season.
